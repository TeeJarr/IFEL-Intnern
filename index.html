Welcome to IFEL!

We're so glad you're here. This interactive guide is your companion to a rewarding and impactful summer internship. Let's get started!

Begin Your Journey

🎯 Set Clear Goals

This section helps you create a roadmap for your summer. Setting clear goals ensures your time is purposeful and aligns your personal ambitions with IFEL's objectives, setting the stage for a successful internship.

Identify Learning Objectives

  • What skills do you want to develop?
  • What knowledge do you want to gain about IFEL?

Discuss with Your Supervisor

  • Align your expectations with IFEL's needs.
  • Ensure projects contribute to your development.

Define Deliverables

  • What concrete projects or tasks will you complete?
  • This provides a sense of accomplishment and demonstrates your contributions.

✨ Generate Personalized Internship Goals

Tell us about your interests or what you hope to focus on, and get personalized goal suggestions!

🚀 Be Proactive and Engaged

Your internship is what you make of it. Taking initiative shows your commitment and maximizes your learning. This section outlines how to actively shape your experience and become a valued team member.

Ask Questions

It shows you're engaged and eager to learn. Don't be afraid to ask "why" to understand the bigger picture.

Seek Opportunities

If you finish a task, ask for more work or inquire if there are other areas where you can assist.

Volunteer for Tasks

Offer to help on interesting projects to learn new skills and expand your network.

Attend Meetings

Participate to understand organizational priorities, challenges, and the context of your work.

Take Notes

Keep a notebook handy to jot down important information, tasks, and key takeaways from discussions.

🤝 Network and Build Relationships

Building a professional network is one of the most valuable outcomes of an internship. The connections you make can provide mentorship and opportunities for years to come. Here's how to get started.

Connect with Colleagues

Introduce yourself to everyone. Learn about their roles and how their work contributes to IFEL's mission.

Schedule Informational Interviews

Ask colleagues for a brief chat about their career paths, roles, and advice. A great way to gain insights.

Attend IFEL Events

Internal or external events, workshops, or webinars offer valuable exposure and networking opportunities.

Leverage LinkedIn

Connect with IFEL staff on LinkedIn to maintain your professional network after your internship concludes.

🛠️ Develop Professional Skills

An internship is a prime opportunity to transition from academic knowledge to professional application. This section highlights key skills to focus on that are valuable in any career path.

Communication: Practice clear, concise communication and professional email etiquette.

Time Management: Learn to prioritize tasks, manage deadlines, and organize your workload.

Problem-Solving: Brainstorm solutions before immediately asking for help.

Adaptability: Be open to new ideas, feedback, and changes in project direction.

Technical Skills: If IFEL uses unfamiliar software, ask for resources to learn them.

✨ Skill Development Advisor

Enter a professional skill you want to develop, and get actionable tips and resource suggestions.

💬 Seek and Act on Feedback

Feedback is a gift that accelerates your growth. Learning to actively seek, gracefully receive, and act on feedback is a skill that will serve you throughout your entire career. Here's how to make it a habit.

Request Regularly

Don't wait for formal reviews. Periodically ask your supervisor for feedback.

Be Receptive

View constructive criticism as an opportunity to learn and improve.

Demonstrate Improvement

Show you're taking feedback seriously by acting on it.

📝 Document Your Experience

Your internship will fly by. It's crucial to keep a record of your work so you can articulate your achievements in future resumes and interviews. This section provides a framework for tracking your contributions.

Maintain a Project Log

List projects, responsibilities, and your specific contributions.

Collect Work Samples

Save reports, presentations, or other materials you create (ensure confidentiality).

Reflect Regularly

Take time each week to think about what you've learned and challenges you've overcome.

🌍 Understand IFEL's Mission and Impact

To truly excel, it's important to see how your work fits into the bigger picture. Understanding our mission will make your tasks more meaningful and help you speak passionately about your experience.

Learn About IFEL's Programs

Familiarize yourself with our different initiatives and how they contribute to the organization's overall mission.

Understand the "Why"

Connect your daily tasks to IFEL's broader goals and the impact it has. This will make your work more meaningful.

🎉 Enjoy the Experience!

Finally, remember to enjoy your time at IFEL. Internships are about learning, growing, and experiencing a professional environment. Embrace the challenges, celebrate your successes, and make the most of this valuable opportunity.

We're excited to see what you'll achieve this summer!
